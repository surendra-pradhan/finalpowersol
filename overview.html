Overview

The best of the best for everyone

PowerSol is one of the leading CLASS I ELECTRICAL CONTRACTORS established in the year 2009 and carrying out projects in India.It is an envisioned endeavour to bring in a qualified and Quality oriented services in the field of Electrical contracting which brought PowerSol into existence.

Set up in 2009 to render electrical services to commercial building, PowerSol soon branched out into other segments of Factories, Solar Power Plants, Transmission and Distribution of Power.

It is the passion for quality and commitment to delivery schedule, which the large clientele recognizes and this attitude made PowerSol predominent.

In our journey of 3years, we have associated and carried out the most reliable electrical installations and have successfully completed Projects in a wide range of market segments such as Solar Farms, Software Technology Parks, Data Centres, Commercial Complexes. We are proud to say that, we are the preferred choice for some of the most Challenging and Short Time Delivery Projects.
